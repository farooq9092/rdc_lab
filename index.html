Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to your common laboratory and sampling queries

How can I book a home collection service?
You can book by calling our helpline at 062 2881565 or 0313 6811565, using the WhatsApp button, or filling the form in the Contact section of this website.
How long does it take to get laboratory reports?
Most routine tests are reported within 12-24 hours. Specialized tests like Genetic analysis or Histopathology may take 3-7 working days.
Are the test prices fixed across all branches?
Yes, Al-Rasheed Diagnostic Center maintains standardized pricing across all collection centers and for home sampling services.
Can I access my old reports online?
Absolutely. Our patient portal stores your medical history securely. Use your Lab Case Number to log in and view or download any previous report.
